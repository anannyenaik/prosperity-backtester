# Strategy Archive

These are historical Round 1, Round 2, and legacy strategies preserved for reference.

They are not the current submission path. The archived Round 3 v1.2 benchmark is `strategies/archive/round3/r3_algo_v1_2_candidate.py`.

Rejected Round 3 traders are kept under `strategies/archive/round3/rejected/` for documented benchmark reproduction only.

Rejected Round 4 strategy candidates are kept under `strategies/archive/round4/rejected/`. They are replay references, not active submission candidates.

Accepted but superseded Round 4 references are kept under `strategies/archive/round4/accepted/`.

The frozen Round 2 files were moved here without logic edits.
